## Contingency Notification Scenarios

### Scenario: Issue Detected During Launch

**Condition:** Error rate or latency exceeds warning threshold

**Immediate Actions (Within 2 minutes):**
```
1. Incident Commander alerted (PagerDuty)
2. Slack #noc channel notification
3. Status page updated to INVESTIGATING
4. Engineering team paged
```

**Customer Notification (Within 5 minutes):**
```
1. Status page updated with issue description
2. Template 4 (Issue Detected) email sent to customers
3. Enterprise customers contacted by CSM
4. #hermes-launch-2026 Slack channel update
5. Support team briefed on issue + workarounds
```

**Escalation Path:**
```
Threshold exceeded → On-call engineer notified
↓
Unclear cause → SRE + Database engineer escalated
↓
Business impact > 1% → VP Engineering notified
↓
Business impact > 5% → CTO + COO notified
↓
Unable to resolve → Rollback decision prepared
```

---

### Scenario: Rollback Required

**Condition:** Critical issue detected, data integrity risk, or business impact > 10%

**Immediate Actions (Within 2 minutes):**
```
1. VP Engineering makes rollback decision
2. CTO/COO informed immediately (phone call)
3. Incident Commander authorizes rollback
4. Platform Engineer initiates procedures
5. All leadership notified (email + Slack + phone)
```

**Customer Notification (Immediately, within 1 minute):**
```
1. Status page updated to RED: "ROLLING BACK"
2. Template 5 (Rollback Decision) email sent ASAP
3. All channels updated simultaneously (Slack, email, in-app)
4. Enterprise customers receive phone calls
5. Support team: "Rollback in progress, expect service restoration in 30 minutes"
```

**Communication Frequency During Rollback:**
```
Every 5 minutes: Status page update
Every 5 minutes: Slack #hermes-incident-[ID] update
Every 10 minutes: Email to customer distribution list
Every 15 minutes: 1-on-1 calls to enterprise customers
Every 30 minutes: Executive briefing call
```
